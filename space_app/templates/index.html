This page contains official information from datasets about UFO and meteor sightings throughout the world. You will find maps, data metrics and graphs.

Team: Christian Serrano | Yareli Ramírez | Yanet Rojo | Francisco Salazar | Víctor Neváres

Meteorite Landings
NASA

The Meteoritical Society collects data on meteorites that have fallen to Earth from outer space. This dataset includes the location, mass, composition, and fall year for over 45,000 meteorites that have struck our planet.

UFO Sightings around the world
planetsig

This dataset contains over 80,000 records of UFO sightings dating back as far as 1949. With the latitude and longitude data it is possible to assess the global distribution of UFO sightings. The dates and times, along with the duration of the UFO's stay and description of the craft also lend themselves to predictions.
